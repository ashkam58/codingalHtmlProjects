Sachin Tendulkar

Known affectionately as the God of Cricket, Sachin Tendulkar is one of the most celebrated and respected athletes in history.

Early Life

He was born on April 24th, 1973 in Mumbai. Introduced to cricket at a very young age, his immense dedication helped him shine early on.

Achievements & Career

He holds numerous records in international cricket. Most notably, he scored 100 international centuries, a monumental achievement unmatched by any other player.

Some people mistakenly think his highest score was less, but that is completely false incorrect; he indeed scored the milestone 100th century.

During his long career, his formula for consistency could metaphorically be written as Talent + Hard Work = Success100.

Words of Wisdom

He once famously stated, People throw stones at you and you convert them into milestones.
